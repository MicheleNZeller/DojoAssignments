About me:

I have been making costumes and props since 2012, my first creation I got to walk across the stage at the 2013 Blizcon. A dream I had since my first Blizcon in 2007. To this day it was still the best day of my life. I hope to do it again someday when the craziness of my life finally settles down to the point I actually have time to make props again.
I play many games! I've been a diehard gamer pretty much since we got out first game station when the N64 came out (having used our neighbors Super Nintentdo forever to the point my parents finally broke down and got me and my younger brother our own.) and have been a diehard gamer ever since.

Currently I am not working on anything new as in my new living quarters limits my space to work, as well as my school severely limits my time and money. :(
